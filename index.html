Moving to America

Hello, my name is Seung Park. My family is originally from Korea, but we immigrated to the states (specifically Alabama) right before I turned 1 years old.

Entering Highschool

My family finally decided to move to Washington state when I was entering into highschool. During my highschool years, I performed in a play, swam in my school's swim team, and sang in the top choir. As the time for graduation approached, I decided to apply to the University of Puget Sound.

Turning Another Chapter In My Life

Upon arriving at UPS I aspired to major in music, but I later decided to switch to become an International Political Economy major. However, I again decided to finally switch majors into Computer Science. I have always tried to run away from all things STEM, but I ended up falling in love with Computer Science.
