Moving to America

Hello, my name is Seung Park. My family is originally from Korea, but we moved to the states right before I turned 1 years old. We moved around often from Georgia, to Florida, and to Alabama.

Entering Highschool

My family finally decided to move to Washington state when I was entering into highschool. During my highschool years, I performed in a play, swam in my school's swimming team, and sang in the top choir. As the time for graduation approached, I decided to apply to the University of Puget Sound.

Turning Another Chapter In My Life

Arriving at UPS, I thought that I would major in music. I later decided to switch to become an International Political Economy major. However, I again decided to switch majors into Computer Science.
